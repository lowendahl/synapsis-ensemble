# Ensemble — Voice & Tone

## In one sentence

Ensemble talks like a quiet stage manager: precise, low-volume, gets out of the way. Never the star.

## Do

- **Plain.** "Open a workspace." Not "Embark on your AI-powered productivity journey."
- **Specific.** "Restored 12 turns from previous session." Not "Welcome back!"
- **Honest.** "Clawpilot not detected — voices will run in mock mode." Not "Some features may be limited."
- **Small letters.** Eyebrow text in `CSU · SYMBIONT` caps; everything else sentence case.
- **Verbs > nouns.** "Send to all" not "Broadcast Submission".

## Don't

- No emoji decoration in headings (`# Workspace` not `# 🚀 Workspace`).
- No exclamation marks. Ever.
- No generic AI hype words: *unleash*, *empower*, *revolutionize*, *seamlessly*, *cutting-edge*.
- No metaphors that pretend the agent is a person ("ux-critic is thinking about your message…"). Status text says what's actually happening: `running…`, `streaming…`, `done`.
- No "we" — Ensemble doesn't have a marketing voice. It's a workbench.

## Examples

| Surface | Bad | Good |
|---|---|---|
| Empty recents | "Looks like you're new! Let's get started." | "No workspaces yet." |
| Send button | "🚀 Launch broadcast!" | "Send to all ▶" |
| Error toast | "Oops! Something went wrong 😔" | "Folder no longer exists" |
| Voice pending | "ux-critic is pondering…" | "thinking" |
| Doctrine pill | "📚 Knowledge base activated!" | "📚 clawpilot/ loaded · tier 1 · auto-injected to all voices" |

## Music reference

The whole product is named after ensemble music. Tone follows: chamber music, not symphony. Dry recording, not reverb.
